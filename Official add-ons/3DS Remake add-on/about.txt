This add-on turns the world into the 3DS Remake version of the game.

Strobulb flashight option added.
New gallery added, with platinum frames, S Rank, Small ghost and Boo portrait, trophy and tasks, and portrait battles.
Temper Terrors, Flashes and Blue Blazes that get shined on by the flashlight will get smug and vanish.
Additional new hidden mansion (later referred to as 3DS Hidden), taking the place of the existing hidden mansion until later unlocked (as GCN Hidden)
